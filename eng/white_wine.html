Our white wines

Fresh and aromatic, our white wine builds on all the potential of the Maria Gomes variety (also known as Fernão Pires). By growing in our rich soils of Monte Real, it can achieve a balanced and unique monovarietal wine.

Created by a process of open-top fermentation, these wines have gained a unique elegance that can only be attributed to the synergy between Maria Gomes and the microclimate of our region.

These wines are ideal to accompany fish dishes, seafood or even a good salad, adding a touch of freshness and lightness to the meal.
