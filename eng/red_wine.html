Our Red Wines

With its grapes hand-picked and vinified following the traditional process, our red wine echoes generations of trial and error, culminating in a product that mirrors all the knowledge acquired through history allied with the modern refinement.

In our vineyards we have planted the Aragonez variety, the Baga variety and the Maria Gomes variety - also known as Fernão Pires - which allows us to make a wide variety of wines.

In addition to winemaking, we also store part of the red wine production for aging in French oak or American oak barrels.

These wines have the ideal profile to accompany red meat dishes, aged cheeses or even game dishes, providing a rich and complex taste experience. We specifically recommend our Duas Castas red wine, with its aging in French oak barrels, to accompany a pork loin or veal in the oven.
