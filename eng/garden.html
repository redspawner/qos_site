Our Garden

The rich terrain of Monte Real that in the past charmed the Portuguese Royal Family and were used as a retreat for kings, now provides nutrients acquired throughout generations, that amass in our fresh products.
Quinta Outeiro da Serva has a garden that produces various vegetables and herbs, all grown organically and sustainably for our consumption. We aim to sell any surplus to interested buyers, or to the local community in order to boost the area's economy and provide high-quality products.
We produce conserves of the products, using the traditional preparation method.

We currently have the following products from our garden available for sale:
• Seasonal vegetables, herbs and fruit, available on prior contact
• Tomato paste from our garden
• Bell pepper paste from our garden
