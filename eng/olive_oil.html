Our olive oil

The olive trees of our estate, with decades of history in their bark, survived throughout several periods, gathering all the richness in the soil, and now providing a flavourful olive oil that can give a drop of the past to your food.
They are grown and maintained with the same care and dedication that we apply to our wines, ensuring that every drop of their oil is a reflection of the quality and tradition of our region.
This olive oil is ideal for dressing salads, enhancing the flavour of fish or meat dishes, or simply for enjoying with fresh-baked bread.
